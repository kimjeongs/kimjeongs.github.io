ABOUT US

We are

An eco-friendly product development solution company that improves environmental issues by converging innovative designs and technologies.

Recently, due to the global issues, the needs of clients and society for eco-friendliness such as carbon reduction and plastic usage reduction are gradually increasing. REVATION is composed of in-house designers and product R&D experts from LG Household & Health Care and HAVE&BE (Dr. Jart+). As the first eco-friendly product R&D SOLUTION specialized group in South Korea, we have eco-friendly marketing, design, and R&D technologies. We aim to become a global green product company with specialized production of eco-friendly products (eco-friendly pulp products, resins, and molding) and specialized R&D processes.

OUR PROCESS

Process

Material

We have capabilities in developing CMF R&D technologies and product development with eco-friendly materials. We have developed paper molds and bioplastic product technologies that improve the shortcomings of pulp mold production methods such as weak strength, easy tearing, high unit prices, and high order quantity to propose an alternative to plastic.

Design

In the design production stage, we produce designs related to products and packaging, and eco-friendly factors and CMFs are considered. As a design strategy, we add elements that strengthen the branding of our customers by emphasizing the value as an eco-friendly packaging through plastic reduction through aesthetic differentiation and application of eco-friendly materials. In addition, we develop the design of eco-friendly products in consideration of the user's convenience and safety.

Manufacturing

The supply and demand of raw materials, prototypes, design, and production are carried out when the production begins, and in the process, design modifications, technology supplements, and production supervision are monitored. We contribute to carbon reduction by producing eco-friendly products that can replace plastic. PACKAGING
Papermold
  • 1. Fiber Treatment
    Dissociate the pulp, bamboo, sugarcane materials.
  • 2. Forming
    Soak it in water, remove it and form it.
  • 3. Designing
    Apply heat to design the shape.
  • 4. Finishing
    Cut the edges cleanly.
  • 5. Packing
    Pack in carton and deliver the product.
PRODUCT
Bio Plastics
  • 1. Fiber Treatment
    Clean and mix the raw materials.
  • 2. Blending
    Blend the color and content.
  • 3. Designing

Product
